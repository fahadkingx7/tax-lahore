Frequently Asked Questions
About Laws

Does all salaried person has to file tax returns?

No, not all salaried person need to file return if they not meet the NTN compulsory filing requirements. Only those salaried person need to file tax return who have Taxable Salary i.e. more than Rs. 50,000.

Requirements for making an NTN Account?

The documents required for Registration on IRIS for creation for NTN are:

  • CNIC Front & Back Sides
  • Mobile Phone not used before on FBR
  • Email Address
  • Residential Address

    • How to become Filer?

    To become a filer you need to file one annual return of the Tax Year 2020 and pay an ATL Surcharge of Rs. 1,000 for Individual, Rs. 10,000 for AOP and Rs. 20,000 for Company. You will remain Tax Filer till 30th March, 2022. If you also file Tax Return of Tax Year 2021 before 30th September, 2021 you will remain Tax Filer till 30th March, 2023.

    What are the requirements to start a Sole Proprietor Business?

    To add a Sole Proprietor Business in NTN of a Tax Payer the following information & Documents are Required:

  • Business Name
  • Business Address
  • Business Purpose or Type
  • Ownership Proof or Rent Deed
  • Electricity Bill
  • Letter Head or Proof of Business

    • Is foreign income is exempt from Tax?

    Not all foreign incomes are exempted from Tax, if the foreign income is already taxed at source, then it cannot be taxed again. however, if the nature of foreign Income is not taxed before or some specific provisions of law are applicable on it, then, such foreign Income is taxable in Pakistan.

    What are requirements for exemption of IT-sector?

    The exemption of IT Sector provided under Section 133 of ITO, 2001 has been removed by FInance Bill, 2021 and IT Sector has been provided Tax Cradit instead. The following are the requirements for Tax Credit provided under 65-F:
    Income from exports of computer software or IT services or IT enabled services as defined in clause (30AD) and (30AE) of section 2 upto the period ending on the 30th day of June, 2025:subject to fulfillment of the following conditions, where applicable, namely:

  • Income tax return has been filed
  • withholding tax statements for the relevant tax year have been filed in respect of those provisions of the Ordinance, where the person is a withholding agent; and
  • sales tax returns for the tax periods corresponding to relevant tax year have been filed if the person is required to file Sales Tax Return under any of the Federal or Provincial sales tax laws.

    • What business type shall be preferred Proprietorship, AOP, Company, LLC?

    The business type depends on the nature of your business & capital, if the business is rather smaller, with small number of employees and there are not Legal requirements then proprietorship may be preferred. If the business has medium capital, there are more than one business members but less than 20 then partnership may be preferred. If the business is large or expected to grow large then the company registered in SECP may be preferred.

    What is the due dates of tax filing?

    FBR has already announced the Individual Tax Payers date of Tax filing is 30th September, 2021. However, for limited companies the due date 31st December, 2021.
